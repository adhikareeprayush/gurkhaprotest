a few words about us

Welcome to Gurkha Pro-Fitness Training Center PVT. LTD., where we provide comprehensive training and support for individuals seeking to join the British Army or Singapore Police force. Our state-of-the-art facility offers a range of services, including hostel accommodation, regular educational classes, interview preparation, physical training and health checkups. Our experienced trainers and instructors are dedicated to helping you achieve your goals and succeed in your journey to becoming a member of the armed forces. So come and join us, and get ready to take the first step towards an exciting and rewarding career.

In addition to the physical and educational components, the training center also offers counseling services and interview preparation to help candidates overcome any challenges they may face during the selection process.

Your Success is our mission

Gurkha Pro-Fitness Training Center is dedicated to helping individuals prepare for their journey to become part of the British Army or Singapore Army. They believe in providing comprehensive support and guidance to each candidate, from the initial stages of preparation to the final selection process. They offer a range of services, including educational classes, physical training, health checkups, hostel accommodations, and healthy diet options, to ensure that each candidate is fully equipped with the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed.

The goal of the training center is to provide a supportive and empowering environment that allows candidates to reach their full potential and achieve their dreams of serving their country. With the expertise of their experienced trainers, the Gurkha Pro-Fitness Training Center is committed to providing the best possible preparation for those seeking to join the British Army or Singapore Police. Providing Top-notch services and resources

At Gurkha Pro-Fitness Training Center, our primary goal is to provide top-notch resources and services to our clients. We understand that the journey to become a part of the British Army or Singapore Army can be challenging, and it is our mission to make this journey as smooth and successful as possible. Our team of expert trainers, facilities, and resources are carefully chosen and maintained to ensure that each candidate receives the best possible preparation for the selection process.

We strive to stay ahead of the curve by constantly updating our training methods and resources, and by incorporating the latest technology and innovations in fitness and preparation. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond just the physical preparation, as we also provide educational classes, counseling services, and interview preparation to help our clients achieve their goals.

Integrity & Dedication
