  • British Army

    The British Army protects the United Kingdom’s interests at home and abroad, providing a safe and secure environment in which all British citizens can live and prosper.
    Qualifications for British Army Recuirt Intake 2023
    Age Requirements:

    • Age: Minimum 18 and maximum 21 years of age as at 01 Jan 2023.
    • Born between AD 01/01/1999 – 01/01/2002 (BS 17/09/2059 – 17/09/2062)


    Physical Requirements:
    • Height: Minimum 158cm.
    • BMI: Between 18 – 28 (by CS).
    • Eyes: Glasses/Contact Lenses/Laser Surgery Not accepted.
    • Physique: Raised legions or abnormalities preventing military service will not accepted.
    • Teeth: No more than two fault (fillings, gaps or false teeth). No faults with front teeth or canines
    • Sick: An Individual PR who is suffering from transferable diseases will not be accepted.


    Educational Qualifications:
    • For those sitting Nepal SEE from 2016 onwards applying British Army: Min 3 x C and 2 x D grades. Must have C grade in Maths and English.
    • For those sitting Nepal SEE from 2016 onwards applying GCSPF: Min 3 x C and 2 x D grades. Must have C grade in English.
    • For those (British Army or GCSPF) w ith GCSE, O Level or other National Certificates equivalent to SEE must have minimum of 5 subjects with minimum grades of 3 x Cs and 2 x Ds (Must passed English and Math). SEE compartmental pass (private SEE and open learning) will be accepted. N level and any vocational subject passes will not be accepted.
